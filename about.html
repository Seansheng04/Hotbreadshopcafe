About our Cafe

As Ōpōtiki’s busiest café, we offer more than just great coffee – we’re a full bakery and café in one. With extended hours, a wide variety of fresh food, and friendly service, we’re the go-to spot for locals and travelers alike, morning to afternoon.

Our History

Established in 1995, Hot Bread Shop Cafe began as a humble bakery serving fresh, handmade bread to the people of Ōpōtiki. Over the decades, we’ve grown into a beloved local café, offering not only baked goods but also full meals and great coffee. Through the years, one thing has remained the same – our commitment to quality and community.
